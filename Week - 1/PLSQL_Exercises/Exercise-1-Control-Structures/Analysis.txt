Exercise 1: Control Structures
Scenario 1:
Used IF condition and FOR LOOP to identify customers above 60 years and reduce loan interest rate by 1%.
Scenario 2:
Used IF condition and FOR LOOP to identify customers with balance greater than 10000 and mark them as VIP.
Scenario 3:
Used CURSOR and FOR LOOP to fetch loans due within 30 days and print reminder messages.

1. IF Statement
2. FOR LOOP
3. CURSOR
4. DBMS_OUTPUT.PUT_LINE
